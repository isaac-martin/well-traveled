How it works.

By partnering with thousands of travel and travel-related websites across the decision making journey (brands, publishers, blogs, etc), Well Traveled is able to collect millions of data points about the Travelers online path to purchase. This data is aggregated into our data warehouse, where it is stored and analyzed to allow us to better understand consumer trends.

You're then able to log into the Well Traveled dashboard and get a deep look at who your consumers are - waht their travel preferences look like, what content they're consuming and what their path to purchase looks like
